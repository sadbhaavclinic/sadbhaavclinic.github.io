Our Eye Doctor

Dr Dhaval Patel

First AIIMS Graduate Ophthalmologist of Gujarat

Dr. Dhaval Patel is a young and dynamic Eye Surgeon in Motera, Ahmedabad. Dr. Dhaval Patel's native is Kalol, A Small town near Ahmedabad,Gujarat,India. But He is born and brought up in Vadodara. His Schooling and MBBS graduation was completed at Vadodara only.

After completing his MBBS at Baroda Medical College in 2010, he joined prestigious All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi in 2011 for his Ophthalmology Residency. Rajendra Prasad Centre for Ophthalmic Sciences (RPCOS, AIIMS) has been recognized as the Apex Organisation by the Government of India under the National Programme for the Control of Blindness. On completing his Ophthalmology Residency at AIIMS, he joined Centre for Sight, Vadodara in 2014 as Cataract & Refractive (LASIK) Surgeon.

He worked at Centre for Sight for 3 years with surgical experience of more than 2500 phacoemulsification surgeries and more than 500 LASIK surgeries. Based on his precise surgical skills and special interest in teaching surgeries, he was appointed as a Phaco Trainer at Centre for Sight and he has trained more than 35 young doctors of India & abroad in Phaco Surgery. Apart from Phacoemulsification and LASIK, his special interests include Complex Anterior Segment Surgeries, Pterygium Surgery with Diamond Burr, Keratoconus Management, Glaucoma Management and Neuro-ophthalmology. He started his new Venture "Sadbhaav Eye & Dental Clinic" along with his wife Dr. Dhara Patel in Motera, Ahmedabad in July 2017. He is well known among Ophthalmology residents all over India and abroad for his Post-Graduate study materials known as "I-notes, Ophthalmonics and Ophthalmology Explorer" which are widely available and read as ebooks and kindle books on internet. He is a well known blogger amongst Ophthalmologists and his blog "RPC Rounds" is one of the favorite destination for Ophthalmology residents. He is one of the few Ophthalmologists from AIIMS practicing in Gujarat and currently the only AIIMS Ophthalmologist practicing in Ahmedabad.



Our Dentist

Dr Dhara Patel

Dr. Dhara Patel is a young and energetic Endodontist in Motera, Ahmedabad. Dr Dhara Patel's native is Vavol, a village near Gandhinagar. She has born and brought up at Gandhinagar. She has completed her schooling from prestigious Kendriya Vidyalaya.

After completing her BDS from Karnavati School of Dentistry (KSD) in 2011, she went on to persue MDS in Endodontics from Ahmedabad Dental College (ADC) in 2013. She finished her MDS in 2016 and then joined Meera Dental Clinic in Vadodara to expand her practice in different parts of Endodontics and Restorative Dentistry. She has performed more than 700 RCTs. In July 2017, she joined her husband to start their own "Sadbhaav Eye & Dental Clinic" at Motera, Ahmedabad. She is well known in her patients' group for her fine skills and good nature.
